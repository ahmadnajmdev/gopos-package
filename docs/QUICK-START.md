# GoPOS Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with GoPOS in 10 minutes!

---

## Step 1: Login

1. Open your browser and go to your GoPOS URL
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **Login**

---

## Step 2: Know Your Dashboard

After login, you'll see the main dashboard with:

- **Sales Overview** - Today's and monthly totals
- **Quick Actions** - Shortcuts to common tasks
- **Alerts** - Low stock and pending items
- **Recent Activity** - Latest transactions

---

## Step 3: Basic POS Sale (For Cashiers)

### Open Your Shift
1. Click **POS** > **Shift Management**
2. Click **Open Shift**
3. Enter cash in your drawer
4. Click **Confirm**

### Make Your First Sale
1. Click **POS** in the menu
2. **Add products** by:
   - Scanning barcode, OR
   - Typing product name, OR
   - Clicking product image
3. Adjust quantity with +/- buttons
4. Click **Checkout**
5. Select payment method
6. Enter amount received
7. Click **Complete Sale**

### End Your Day
1. Go to **POS** > **Shift Management**
2. Click **Close Shift**
3. Count your cash
4. Enter the amount
5. Click **Confirm**

---

## Step 4: Essential Tasks by Role

### If You're a Manager

**Check Sales:**
- Go to **Sales** > **Sales** to view all transactions

**Check Inventory:**
- Go to **Inventory** > **Products** to see stock levels

**View Reports:**
- Go to **Reports** for sales and inventory reports

### If You're Warehouse Staff

**Receive Inventory:**
1. Go to **Purchases** > **Purchases**
2. Create new purchase with received items

**Transfer Stock:**
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Stock Transfers**
2. Create transfer between warehouses

**Do Stock Count:**
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Stock Counts**
2. Create count and enter actual quantities

### If You're an Accountant

**Record Expenses:**
1. Go to **Accounting** > **Expenses**
2. Click **Create** and fill details

**View Financial Reports:**
- **Reports** > **Income Statement**
- **Reports** > **Balance Sheet**

**Create Journal Entry:**
1. Go to **Accounting** > **Journal Entries**
2. Enter debits and credits (must balance)

### If You're HR Staff

**Record Attendance:**
1. Go to **HR** > **Attendances**
2. View or create attendance records

**Process Leave:**
1. Go to **HR** > **Leave Requests**
2. Review and approve/reject requests

---

## Step 5: Top 5 Things to Remember

1. **Always open a shift** before using POS
2. **Save often** when creating records
3. **Check stock levels** before promising items
4. **Print receipts** for customer transactions
5. **Close your shift** at the end of the day
