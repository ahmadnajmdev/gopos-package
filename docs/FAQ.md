# GoPOS Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

---

## General Questions

### What is GoPOS?
GoPOS is a comprehensive Point of Sale and Business Management System designed for retail businesses. It includes POS, inventory management, accounting, and HR modules.

### What browsers are supported?
GoPOS works best on:
- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari

### Can I use GoPOS on mobile devices?
Yes, GoPOS is responsive and works on tablets. For POS operations, we recommend using a tablet in landscape mode or a desktop/laptop computer.

### What languages does GoPOS support?
- English
- Arabic (العربية)
- Kurdish Sorani (کوردی)

You can change your language in your profile settings.

## Account & Login

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

### Why can't I access certain features?
Your access is determined by your role. Contact your administrator if you need additional permissions.

### How do I change my password?
1. Click on your name (top-right corner)
2. Select **Profile**
3. Go to **Password** section
4. Enter current and new password

## POS Operations

### Why can't I use the POS?
Make sure you have:
1. POS access permission (Cashier, Manager, or Admin role)
2. An open shift (go to **POS** > **Shift Management** > **Open Shift**)

### How do I process a refund?
1. Go to **Sales** > **Sale Returns**
2. Click **Create Return**
3. Select the original sale
4. Choose items to return
5. Process the refund

### Can I split payment between cash and card?
Yes! During checkout:
1. Click **Split Payment**
2. Enter cash amount
3. Add card payment for remaining
4. Complete the sale

### How do I hold a sale for later?
1. Add products to cart
2. Click **Hold Sale**
3. Enter a note (e.g., customer name)
4. To recall: Click **Held Sales** and select it

### Why can't I see certain products in POS?
Products may not appear if:
- They are marked as inactive
- They have zero stock (if inventory tracking is enabled)
- They are not assigned to your warehouse

### How do I apply a discount?
During checkout:
1. Click **Discount**
2. Enter percentage or fixed amount
3. The discount is applied to the total

### What is a Z-Report?
A Z-Report (or shift report) summarizes all transactions during a shift, including:
- Total sales
- Payment method breakdown
- Cash movements
- Variance (expected vs. counted cash)

## Inventory

### How do I check product stock?
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Products**
2. Find your product
3. Stock quantity is shown in the list or product detail

### How do I add new products?
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Products**
2. Click **Create Product**
3. Fill in name, SKU, price, etc.
4. Click **Save**

### What's the difference between batch and serial tracking?

**Batch Tracking:**
- Groups products by production lot
- Tracks expiry dates
- Good for food, medicine, cosmetics

**Serial Tracking:**
- Each unit has unique serial number
- Tracks individual items
- Good for electronics, equipment

### How do I transfer stock between warehouses?
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Stock Transfers**
2. Click **Create Transfer**
3. Select source and destination warehouses
4. Add products and quantities
5. Submit for approval
6. After approval, complete the transfer

### What do I do if stock count doesn't match?
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Stock Counts**
2. Create a stock count
3. Enter actual quantities
4. Review variances
5. Post to adjust inventory automatically

### How do I set up low stock alerts?
1. Edit the product
2. Find **Low Stock Alert** field
3. Enter minimum quantity
4. Save

When stock falls below this level, an alert appears.

## Accounting

### How do I record an expense?
1. Go to **Accounting** > **Expenses**
2. Click **Create**
3. Select expense type
4. Enter amount and date
5. Save

### How do I create a journal entry?
1. Go to **Accounting** > **Journal Entries**
2. Click **Create Entry**
3. Add debit and credit lines
4. **Important:** Debits must equal credits
5. Save

### Where can I see financial reports?
Go to **Reports** and select:
- **Balance Sheet** - Assets, liabilities, equity
- **Income Statement** - Revenue and expenses
- **Trial Balance** - All account balances
- **Cash Flow** - Cash movements

### How do I manage tax rates?
1. Go to **Accounting** > **Tax Codes**
2. Create or edit tax codes
3. Assign to products as needed

## Human Resources

### How do I add a new employee?
1. Go to **HR** > **Employees**
2. Click **Create Employee**
3. Fill in personal and employment details
4. Save

### How do I record attendance?
1. Go to **HR** > **Attendances**
2. Click **Create Attendance**
3. Select employee and enter times
4. Save

### How do I process leave requests?
1. Go to **HR** > **Leave Requests**
2. Find pending requests
3. Review and click **Approve** or **Reject**

### How do I run payroll?
1. Go to **HR** > **Payroll Periods**
2. Click **Create Period**
3. Select month/year
4. Click **Generate Payslips**
5. Review and approve
6. Process payment

### How are employee loans handled?
1. Create loan in **HR** > **Employee Loans**
2. Approve the loan
3. System automatically deducts installments from monthly payroll

## Reports

### How do I export reports?
1. Generate the report
2. Click **Export**
3. Select format (PDF, Excel, CSV)

### Why is my report showing no data?
Check:
1. Date range is correct
2. Filters aren't excluding your data
3. Transactions exist for that period
4. You have permission to view this data

### Can I customize reports?
Standard reports have fixed formats. For custom reports, contact your system administrator.

## Troubleshooting

### The page won't load
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection

### My changes aren't saving
1. Check for validation errors (red text)
2. Ensure all required fields are filled
3. Try refreshing and re-entering

### The system is slow
1. Close other browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet speed
4. Contact administrator if issue persists

### I made a mistake on a transaction
- For sales: Create a sale return
- For purchases: Create a purchase return
- For journal entries: Create a reversing entry
- Contact your manager or administrator for help

## Security

### How often should I change my password?
We recommend changing your password every 90 days.

### What makes a strong password?
- At least 8 characters
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase
- Include numbers
- Include special characters (!@#$%)

### Should I share my account?
**Never share your login credentials.** Each user should have their own account for security and audit purposes.
